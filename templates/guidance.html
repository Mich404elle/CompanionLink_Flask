Training Guidance

General Guidelines

  • The goal of the Companion Call program is to socialize and build meaningful friendships.
  • Throughout your time with CompanionLink, adhere to your volunteer rights and responsibilities.
  • Do not give advice or meet your companion in person.
  • If uncertain, reach out to CompanionLink's Volunteer Coordinator.
  • Keep copies of the Volunteer Handbook & Agreements for reference.
  • Report any Breach of Confidentiality to your Supervisor immediately.

Communication Standards

  • Effective Communication
    • Introduce yourself each time
    • Speak clearly and annunciate carefully
    • Be patient and listen actively
  • Accommodation Practices
    • Check for assistance devices
    • Use video calls when possible
    • Utilize large font when typing
  • Creating Positive Atmosphere
    • Maintain friendly tone
    • Stay flexible and engaging
    • End calls positively

Prohibited Actions

  • Never interrupt or correct your companion
  • Avoid impatient or raised voices
  • Do not use patronizing speech (Elderspeak)
